Working Papers

How to Achieve Technological Leapfrogging: Evidence from China's Self-Strengthening Movement
Originally titled: Cannon to Kilowatts: The Enduring Legacy of the Self-Strengthening Movement on China's Electrification
R&R at Cliometrica
Accepted by The Eleventh Annual Symposium on Quantitative History
Abstract: This paper examines how China's Self-Strengthening Movement (1861-1895) contributed to technological advancement and economic development, providing insights into mechanisms of technological leapfrogging in developing economies. Through historical analysis and quantitative methods, we explore the enduring impact of this modernization effort on China's subsequent electrification and industrial development.
